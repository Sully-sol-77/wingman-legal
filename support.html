Common Topics

Before contacting support, you may find answers to common questions below:

Billing & Subscriptions

  • Subscriptions are managed through the Apple App Store
  • To cancel your subscription, go to your Apple App Store account settings
  • For refunds, please contact Apple Support or use the App Store's refund process
  • We do not process refunds directly

Login & Account Issues

  • If you've forgotten your password, use the password reset feature in the app
  • For account access issues, please contact us with your email address
  • We can help you recover or delete your account if needed

Feature Requests

  • We welcome your feedback and suggestions
  • Please email us with details about the feature you'd like to see
  • While we can't implement every request, we review all feedback

Bug Reports

  • If you encounter a bug, please email us with:
  • Description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the problem
  • Your device and app version information
  • Screenshots if applicable

Response Time

We aim to respond to all support inquiries within 1–2 business days. For urgent issues, please include "URGENT" in your subject line.
